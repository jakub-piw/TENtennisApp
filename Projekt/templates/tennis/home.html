Welcome to TENtenis club!

The TENTennis Club is a premier tennis facility located in the heart of the city. Founded in 1995, it has been a fixture in the community for years. It features 12 outdoor courts, 4 indoor hard courts. The club also offers a variety of programs and clinics for players of all ages and skill levels, as well as a pro shop, lounge area and fitness center. The TENTennis Club is known for its friendly and welcoming atmosphere, making it a great place for both serious players and casual enthusiasts. The Elmwood Tennis Club is a great choice for anyone looking to improve their game or simply enjoy a day of tennis with friends and family.

As a member of the TENTennis Club, you'll have access to the club's top-notch facilities and programming. You'll be able to play on the club's courts, take part in the many programs and clinics offered, and utilize the practice wall, fitness center, and pro shop. The club also offers a variety of leagues and tournaments for members to participate in, providing opportunities to compete and challenge yourself against players of all skill levels. The club events and tournaments also creates great opportunity for members to socialize and make new friends with other tennis enthusiasts.

The TENTennis Club is proud to announce that it has recently been awarded the Golden Racket award in "Best Tennis Club" category by the National Tennis Association. This prestigious honor recognizes the club's exceptional facilities, programming, and overall commitment to promoting and growing the sport of tennis in the community. The award is a testament to the hard work and dedication of the club's staff, coaches, and volunteers. The award win is a great achievement for the club, and serves as a validation of the club's efforts to provide the best tennis experience for its members and the community. The club's management and the staffs are thrilled to receive the recognition and will continue to work hard to make the TENTennis Club the go-to destination for tennis players in the region.
